Our department initiated a series of events


2 Day SDN Hands On Workshop
Chandigarh University

When we started working on SDN 5-6 years back, not much information or help was available regarding SDN. Getting started with this emerging concept was very difficult. It was really very painful process. Now we do not want this to happen to the persons who at this point of time want to enter into this field. This workshop starts from basics & takes you to advance levels of Software Defined Networking. According to industry people and research scholars, SDN is going to redefine networking and cloud world. This is the biggest thing that has happened in networking field in last 30 years. SDN is a New Way to Design, Build and Operate Networks. There is a Need to Innovate in the Network. We’ve seen tons of innovation in applications, devices, computing and storage, yet the underlying network that connects everything has remained unchanged. SDN Opens the Network Up for Innovation. It replaces static, inflexible and complex networks, with networks that are agile, scalable and innovative.

Timings: 9.30 AM – 5.30 PM
Schedule: October 10, 11, 2018

Max. Participants: 20

Pricing: Rs. 12000

Duration: 2 Days

Venue: Chandigarh University, Gharuan, Mohali

Conducted by: Chandigarh University & UNET Labs Private Limited

Experts: Vipin Gupta, Sukhveer Kaur & Karamjeet Kaur

Objectives

After attended this workshop you will be able to:
• Understand how to setup SDN Lab

• Understand how to run POX controller, MininetEmulator, Wireshark

• Understand how to create Topologies in Mininet

• Understand Mininet commands

• Understand how to create SDN Applications without Controller

• Understand how to use POX SDN controller with Mininet Topologies

• Understand how to create applications using POX Controller

• Creating Hub, Switch & Firewall Applications

• Running SDN Applications on Real Hardware
